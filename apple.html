Product Description

Our Honeycrisp apples are known for their explosive crunch and perfect balance of sweetness and acidity. Grown in the nutrient-rich volcanic soil of the Pacific Northwest, these apples are left on the tree until peak ripeness to ensure maximum flavor development. Each apple is hand-picked and inspected for the perfect red-blush color.

Farming Practices

Sustainable Orchards


We utilize predatory insects for natural pest control and complex irrigation systems to minimize water waste by 30% compared to industrial farms.

Traceability


Scan the QR code on your bag to see the exact plot where your apples were harvested and the date of picking.

Storage Tips


Refrigeration: Keep in the crisper drawer to maintain crunch for up to 3 weeks.

Separation: Keep away from strong-smelling foods as apples absorb odors.
